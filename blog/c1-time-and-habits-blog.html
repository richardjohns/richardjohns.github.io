    My reflections on time and habit techniques

    Enspiral blog 1 - 16th July 2017

    Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

    In my own accounting career over the last year I've tended to use pomodoro already, just in an hour-long format so 50 minutes on, 10 off to recharge. It seems to be my natural style and helps with sustaining energy levels through the day (or at least until I get a proper coffee!)

    What did you find interesting?

    I liked the emphasis on reflection. I'll tend to use more of an email 'get things done' approach to collect everything each morning and plan the week, but I don't stop for proper reflection time though um not really a lot of learning going on there. However in my 10 minutes off for pomodoro I'd often reflect on what was happening over coffee which often resulted in my realising the way forward on a piece of work, or to think of the bigger picture. I'm interested in trying it as part of my course learnings and seeing how that goes.

    What worked for you?

    I thought the discussion on acting on implementing a big vision by starting with a small mvp first was really helpful and focussed my attention on what really matters for some ideas I've been thinking over

    Which will you experiment further with?

    I'm really looking forward to understanding more about mindfulness per the article and 'search inside yourself'. I can see the benefits are possible from friends alot further on in that area so looking forward to trying it.
