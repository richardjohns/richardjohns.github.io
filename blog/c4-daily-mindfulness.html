Read or watch chapter 3 of Search Inside Yourself, and reflect:

Q: What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

My experiences of mindfulness conversation at day-in-the-life impacted me so I've started to make these moments a part of daily conversations at work. Any time I feel I'm dipping out cause of pressure or stress I try dropping into being a bit more present and forgetting other issues. At least my conversation can then wind up being productive and the relationship maintained!

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

Personally I resort to mindful conversations as above now, and I'm well into starting on the meditation sessions in the Headspace app.

Q: Any other musings?

I appreciate the help with getting into a routine that Headspace provides. Without it I think I'd struggle to keep the discipline up. It motivates to see my progress through the packs and being able to focus on certain issues gives direction to my meditations. Just starting out but think it's well thought out.
