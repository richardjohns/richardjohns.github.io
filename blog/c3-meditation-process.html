Q: How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

Hehe. I think overall it's hard to avoid admitting that this approach was necessary to survive the exercise at all... my sites certainly have elements that don't move as they should and I reached the end of my tether trying to resolve those issues and was forced to accept 'process over product'. lol

Read or watch chapter 2 of Search Inside Yourself, and reflect:

Q: What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

A: Essentially I had a pre-conception that it was more to do with emptying the mind, which isn't really true - more about redirecting it or learning to focus it.

Q: What new things have you learnt about meditation?

A: One of the more important ideas to me is that the exercises help me train my 'meta-attention', meaning that I will eventually be able to change my mental focus so that I can calmly examine my own internal state eg whether happy or sad in a calm way which would then lead to greater control.

Q: Any other musings?

A: Yes in that I've had some big interviews over the last week that were probably the most successful I've completed, and I can partly credit mindfulness techniques for that. I'm usually a ball of nerves when attending these, but last week after preparing with mindfulness meditations I was able to put myself in a calm, focussed state for both interviews - completely chuffed when I walked out.
