      Reflections on Sprint-2

      Enspiral blog 7 - 23rd July 2017

      What did I do well this week?

      This week I was really proud to move the website forward and fix some layout issues with the header which created a cleaner look for the website. It took alot of wrestling with the unsplash image as a background image and getting to grips with inspector to make it happen. Then I started referring to some of the bootstrap example sites and figuring out how the classes worked and could be overridden as needed. Still very much hit and miss getting anything to work! It's not part of the course materials but I was a bit concerned that I wouldn't have any way to make some of my wishlist ideas come to life this week, so I started building my understanding. Now I feel a lot more comfortable like parts of the list are achievable.

      What could I do to improve?

      Well, on the other hand I needed to get the balance right in terms of completing assignments as these are not quite done in time. Getting lost in the appeal of nailing a website needs to be balanced with a drive to complete assignments first before getting to play!
