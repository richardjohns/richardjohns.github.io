      Enspiral blog 7 - 23rd July 2017

      Read or watch chapter 1 of Search Inside Yourself, and reflect:

      • Q: What learnings did you take from the chapter?
      • A: In this chapter Chade talks about the importance of emotional intelligence (EQ) even over technical or intellectual intelligence (IQ) in order to succeed in the workplace, and makes the point that through the exercises in the book the mind can be trained and develop EQ. This EQ leads to greater success in our work, outstanding leadership, and accesses more happy states of being too which impacted me.

      • Q: How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?
      • A: I've tried it over the last week and find it beneficial. I'm getting into the idea of focussing attention on the present through my breath and just being. When the session ends I feel almost like I've come out of a light sleep as it's restful.
