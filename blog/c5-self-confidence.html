Read or watch chapter 4 of Search Inside Yourself, and reflect:

Q: What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

A: Self awareness is defined in the book as “knowing one’s internal states, preferences, resources and intuitions", and is considered as the key enabler of the other emotional intelligences. Mindfulness helps train our ability to focus and understand our internal states in a non-judgemental way, and even how we bounce back from difficulties or setbacks. This greater focus and understanding is said to lead to self-confidence.

Q: Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

A: I tried body-scanning which is a daily exercise in the Headspace app. It involves focusing attention slowly from the top of the head, down the body to the feet, and acknowledging how the parts of the body feel. The main thing that seems to happen is usually a release of tension as the scan often reveals a lot of this building up in the body.

Q: What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

A: I took away the main conclusion that it's possible to gain mastery over our own emotional state, and choose whether to act of them, and sometime s even whether to feel them.
